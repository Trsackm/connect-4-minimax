The Rules of Connect 4



The rules of Connect 4 are very simple. You will be playing a game against a computer player. Each round, you will have to add a piece to a column and the computer will do the same. The pieces fall to the bottom of the board and stack up. To add a piece to a column, click on a cell in the column.

The game ends when there are either four in a row, a column, any diagonal of the same color, or when there are no more legal moves.

This computer player uses a recursive algorithm to find the optimal move. It may take time for it to find the right move. So don't click too many times if there is some lag.

You can set the difficulty with the "difficulty" slider and you can reset the game by clicking on the "reset" button. I hope you enjoy this game.
